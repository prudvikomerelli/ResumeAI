## Problem Statement
Job seekers struggle to tailor their resumes for each application, often getting filtered out by Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) that scan for specific keywords and formatting. ResumeAI solves this by using AI to transform a generic resume into an ATS-optimized, job-specific resume and tailored cover letter — complete with match scoring and keyword gap analysis. This eliminates the tedious manual process of customizing applications and significantly increases the chances of getting past automated screening.
